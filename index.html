Specifications

Power

Boasting the most powerful road engine ever installed by McLaren, the Senna outputs an impressive 789 horsepower with a maximum torque of 590 pound-feet.

Design

The design has been radically reimagined from its previous designs, such as the P1. The Senna features a bold, aggressive appearance that reflects its aerodynamic properties.

Innovation

The pioneering of the Senna goes much further than appearances alone. Extensive use of carbon fibre, active aerodynamics and revolutionary suspension technology have been intricately pulled together to deliver more than 800kg of downforce.

The Legend

Considered to be one of the greatest F1 racers of all time, Ayrton Senna accomplished three world championships, 41 race wins and 65 pole positions. As a testament to his competitive nature and indomitable spirit, his legend will continue to remain at McLaren.
